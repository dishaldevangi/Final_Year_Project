Heart Attack Prediction using Retinal Images

Abstract


ABSTRACT
In this project, we propose an intelligent system to detect and distinguish Vitamin Deficiency from Human Tissue by deep learning techniques. At first, images are clustered to segment out the lesion. Our aim is to test the effectiveness of the proposed segmentation technique, extract the most suitable features, and compare the classification results with other methods. A deep neural network is then utilized for the classification of stomach cancer images.
